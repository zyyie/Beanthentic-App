FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Go to Account Security and fill out the password reset form. Enter your current password, then provide a new password (minimum 6 characters).
What is Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?
Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security. After enabling 2FA, you will need to enter a code from your authenticator app in addition to your password.
How do I set up 2FA?
  1. Open Account Security.
  2. Click Enable 2FA.
  3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app.
  4. Save backup recovery codes.
What are backup recovery codes?
Backup codes are one-time use codes you can use to access your account if you lose your authenticator device. Keep them in a secure place.
How can I view my activity log?
Open Activity Log. Use the search bar to filter by date, action, details, or IP address.
