Guides for farmers

  • Beanthentic helps Lipa coffee farmers register farms, keep records, and support Kapeng Barako traceability.

    1. Home — news, shortcuts, and quick access to messages.
    2. Record — view and manage your farmer records.
    3. Register — add or update farm registration details.
    4. History — see past transactions and activity.
    5. Account — your profile, settings, and this Help page.

    Tip: Use the bottom menu on every screen to move between these areas quickly.

  • Complete registration so your farm appears in Beanthentic and can be verified for GI readiness.

    1. Tap Register on the bottom menu (or open Register from Home).
    2. Fill in farm details: location, variety (e.g. Barako), area, and other required fields.
    3. Upload clear photos of documents or farm proof when asked.
    4. Review everything, then tap Submit.
    5. Wait for confirmation — you can check status under Register or your registration summary.

    Tip: Good internet is needed when submitting. If upload fails, try again on a stronger connection.

  • Your data stays organized so buyers and partners can trust your coffee origin.

    1. Open Record to see your farmer profile and linked farm data.
    2. Each registered farmer can have a QR code for quick access during field visits or sales.
    3. Use History to review transactions and past updates.
    4. Keep records accurate — update registration if your farm details change.

    Tip: Do not share your login. Only share QR or public profile details when you intend to.

  • Protect your account and control how the app works on your phone.

    1. Go to Account → Account Settings → Settings.
    2. Security — change your password; use a strong password you do not share.
    3. Notifications — turn alerts on or off (login, security, app updates).
    4. General — language, light/dark theme, and app version info.
    5. Update your profile photo and personal info under Account Settings when needed.

    Tip: Forgot your password? On the login screen, use the help link, or change password in Settings after signing in.

  • Beanthentic collects farm and account information to support registration and traceability under Philippine data privacy rules.

    • We use your data to manage your farmer profile, registration, and app features.
    • Do not give your password to anyone — staff will not ask for it.
    • You can read the full Privacy Notice from Account Settings when you need legal details.

    Tip: Only enter true farm information. Wrong data can delay verification.

  • Try these steps before asking for support:

    1. Check your internet connection (Wi‑Fi or mobile data).
    2. Refresh the page or close and reopen the app.
    3. Sign out, then sign in again with the correct account.
    4. Make sure the app has permission for camera/storage if uploading photos.
    5. If registration is pending, wait for admin review — status may update later.

    Still stuck? Use Contact Us or ask your Beanthentic project administrator.
